Uco Valley

Acknowledged worldwide, Uco Valley is the birthplace of the best Malbec wines from Argentina. On top of a dazzling nature at the foothills of the Andes range, the area offers culinary, sports, religious and historical circuits that captivate the visitors of this fertile and picturesque land.

Acknowledged worldwide, Uco Valley is the birthplace of the best Malbec wines from Argentina. On top of a dazzling nature at the foothills of the Andes range, the area offers culinary, sports, religious and historical circuits that captivate the visitors of this fertile and picturesque land.

Title

The vineyards that give birth to Tamarí wines are located between 900 and 1100 meters above sea level, right at the foothills of the Andes range. There, 100 km south of the city of Mendoza, the climate is continental dry. This allows a wide temperature range with very hot days and very cold nights. Average annual temperature is 14°C, and yearly rainfall is about 300 mm.

Title

The soil is alluvial, only a few centimeters deep, of sandy and fine rocky structure. Very poor in organic matter, with low capacity to retain humidity and poor natural fertility.

Title

The terroir´s particular characteristics allow the production of high quality grapes with excellent health. To this we add the knowledge, the careful and delicate handcraft of the women harvesters and all the experience of Andrea Ferreyra, the winery’s enologist.

Title

So the sun, the land and the water combine with a passionate spirit for self-improvement to create Tamarí, a wine with the flavor of the mountains and the sky.
